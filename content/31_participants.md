
# Participants

Participants define who sends and who receives money.

## Customer

Represents the sender of funds.

Used in:

- Payments

## Beneficiary

Represents the receiver of funds.

Used in:

- Payouts (bank)
- Cash withdrawals

Beneficiary is the payout destination, not a cash-specific concept.


> Beneficiaries are used for all payout rails (bank, cash, and proxy-key schemes like PIX).
